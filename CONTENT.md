For shops, wholesalers, and any business with stock and staff. Features: sales & POS tracking, stock control with loss alerts, expense tracking, customer records & repeat-sale follow-up, daily reports to your phone, staff accounts with permissions.

**SmartPharmacy**; *Every tablet accounted for.*
For pharmacies. Features: AI-powered audit that flags suspicious stock movement, automatic stock reports, expiry tracking, sales & prescriptions records, online catalogue your customers can browse, daily summary to the owner's phone.

**SmartHotels**; *Full house, zero chaos.*
